Protag Dev Studios Team Member Signup

Thank you for your interest in joining Protag Dev Studios! If you are accepted, you will receive an email containing your PDS ID. You will then be interviewed via email.

Requirements for Joining:

  • Must have a strong understanding of relevant skills. We only want the smart ones.
  • Must possess a proper GameMaker Studio license. Otherwise, how would you help me fix bugs?
  • Must be familiar with the development process. Don't worry, team members get early builds of TPC (DO NOT LEAK)
  • Must have CashApp for payment. Otherwise, you won't have money when TPC sales spike.
  • Must have a valid email address. Otherwise, you won't know your PDS ID.
  • Must provide a contactable phone number.

Rules for Being a Dev at Protag Dev Studios:

  • Do not modify game project files without prior consent. I can break this rule as I am the lead developer who HAS the project files. You just send ideas and assets into the devs-only channel in the Protag Dev Studios Discord server.
  • Respectful and professional behavior is expected. Okay...maybe some fun is allowed, but serious time means serious time.
  • Adhere to all legal and ethical standards, no criminal activity. We don't need our team members getting arrested.
  • Members with prior criminal records are allowed but must not commit further crimes. We don't want you going back to jail.
  • Age is not a restriction; anyone passionate is welcome, Here at Protag Dev Studios, age is just a number, just don't be a troublemaker.
  • Do not spam or misuse the registration form; there's no point.

Additional Info:

  • Members under the legal hiring age may set their own, custom schedule.
  • Members at the legal hiring age must follow a set schedule (see below).
